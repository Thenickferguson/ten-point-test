The Clubhouse Moorooka — Strategic Framework
The Ten Point Test
Every idea is scored against five principles — two points each, ten in total. Ideas must reach 5 or above to be considered by the President.
The Clubhouse
Moorooka
01
Community Counts
Does it serve people beyond just paying patrons?
Does it appeal to more than one community group?
02
Beyond the Bar
Does it contribute to a revenue pillar other than the bar?
Does it reduce our dependence on Friday/Saturday trade?
03
Members Matter
Does it give existing members a reason to show up?
Does it give non-members a reason to join?
04
Fill the Floor
Does it bring people into a part of the venue that is currently underused?
Does it make use of a space that is currently sitting idle?
05
Self-Sufficient
Can it operate without ongoing volunteer effort?
Can it cover its own costs within 12 months?
Score 0–2 per principle  ·  5 or above progresses to the President  ·  Maximum score 10
